Final Project

What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

The most challenging thing about this project was getting the items to show up how I wanted them to in the grid. It took literal days to figure out how to align the extreme weather page in a way that matched my vision. Through much trial and error I was able to overcome this challenge and used the method that worked to help realign the other pages.

What did you enjoy most about this project?

I enjoyed the creative freedom we were given to both choose what we wanted our website to be about and how we wanted it to look. Since I was creating my own website I was going off of the site plan I created myself. It was nice to have a vision of what I wanted it to look like in the end but it also gave me a chance to explore different Ideas and layouts as I was creating the site.

What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

I would probably try to find ways to make teh website more interactive on all of the pages.
